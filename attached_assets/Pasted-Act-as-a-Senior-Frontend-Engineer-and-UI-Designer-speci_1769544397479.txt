Act as a Senior Frontend Engineer and UI Designer specializing in modern, high-end interfaces. Build a single-page personal holding company website called "Stillfrost".

### THE AESTHETIC: "Ethereal Glassmorphism"
The vibe must be light, airy, futuristic, and premium.
Think: Apple visionOS interface, frozen vapor, clean laboratory.

**Crucial Design Elements:**
1.  **Background:** NOT solid white. It needs a very subtle, soft gradient background to allow the glass cards to stand out. Use a linear gradient from top-left to bottom-right, fading from a very pale icy blue to faint white (e.g., `bg-gradient-to-br from-sky-50 via-white to-indigo-50`).
2.  **The "Frosted Glass" Cards:**
    -   **Background:** High transparency white (`bg-white/40` to `bg-white/60`).
    -   **Blur:** Heavy frosted effect (`backdrop-blur-xl` or `2xl`).
    -   **Border:** A crisp, thin, semi-transparent white border to define edges (`border border-white/50`).
    -   **Shadow:** A very soft, diffuse shadow colored with light blue to lift it off the page (`shadow-xl shadow-sky-200/30`).
    -   **Interaction:** Subtle hover lift (`hover:-translate-y-1`) and a slightly brighter border.

### TECH STACK
- Framework: React (Vite)
- Styling: Tailwind CSS (essential for utility-first glassmorphism)
- Animation: Framer Motion (for smooth, ethereal entrances)
- Icons: Lucide React (use thin stroke icons)
- Fonts: Use Google Fonts.
  - Headings: 'Playfair Display' (Serif) - conveys institutional weight.
  - Body/UI: 'Geist Mono' or 'JetBrains Mono' (Monospace) - conveys technical precision.

### COLOR PALETTE
- **Text Primary:** Deep Slate Blue/Charcoal (e.g., `text-slate-800`). DO NOT use pure black, it's too harsh against the light glass.
- **Text Secondary:** Muted Blue-Grey (e.g., `text-slate-500`).
- **Accents:** Clear Sky Blue (e.g., `text-sky-600`) for links or highlights.
